Project: DMARC, SPF, DKIM Setup

✅ DMARC Setup Implementation – Deployed DMARC, SPF, and DKIM to prevent email spoofing.

Objective:

Protect organizational email infrastructure against spoofing, phishing, and unauthorized sending through DMARC, SPF, and DKIM configuration.

Summary:

  • Audited and implemented DMARC, SPF, and DKIM records for domains hosted on Hostinger and GoDaddy.
  • Reviewed existing DNS records and applied proper TXT configurations with recommended policies (p=quarantine / p=reject).
  • Verified DKIM signing through Microsoft 365 and validated DNS propagation for both domains.
  • Monitored alignment and email delivery reports to fine-tune policy strength over time.

Key Highlights:

  • Successfully mitigated spoofing risks and improved domain reputation
  • Configured DNS across different providers (Hostinger & GoDaddy)
  • Aligned with email security best practices for Microsoft 365
