Project: VLAN and Network Redesign

✅ VLAN Implementation & Network Renovation – Re-architected network with VLANs, switch configuration, rack management, and structured cabling.

Objective:

Modernize and secure the organization's network infrastructure by implementing VLAN segmentation, centralized wireless and traffic management, and professional rack and cabling standards.

Summary:

  • Took full ownership of end-to-end network renovation including physical setup, switch deployment, and logical VLAN design.

  • Designed and executed structured cabling plan with best practices for rack layout, labeling, and cable routing.
  • Reduced broadcast domains and improved network troubleshooting through segmentation and documentation.

Key Highlights:

  • Improved network performance and security via VLAN isolation
  • Implemented professional structured cabling and rack layout
  • Centralized WLAN and traffic monitoring for better control and scalability
