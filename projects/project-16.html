Project: IT & Security Policy Development

✅ Policy Development & Implementation – Created internal IT, security, and compliance policies aligned with best practices.

Objective:

Establish a foundational governance framework by drafting and enforcing internal IT, security, and compliance policies tailored to the organization’s operational needs.

Summary:

  • Developed IT usage, password, email, backup, and data handling policies following industry standards and compliance requirements.
  • Collaborated with management to define acceptable use and escalation processes for IT assets and services.
  • Documented policies clearly for staff onboarding and audits, and enforced consistent adherence across departments.
  • Implemented review and revision cycles to keep all policy documents up to date.

Key Highlights:

  • Aligned policy design with ISO/IEC 27001-inspired security practices
  • Improved organizational compliance readiness and internal IT discipline
  • Enabled smoother onboarding and clearer accountability for users
Skills Demonstrated: IT Governance | Security Policy | Compliance | Documentation | User Education | Process Design
